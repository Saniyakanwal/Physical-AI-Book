# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook. This interactive educational platform is designed to help you learn about the cutting-edge field of Physical AI and humanoid robotics.

## What You'll Learn

- Fundamentals of Robot Operating System (ROS2)
- Simulation environments with Gazebo and Unity
- NVIDIA Isaac robotics platform
- Vision-Language-Action models for robotics
- Practical implementation of humanoid robots

## How to Use This Textbook

This textbook features:
- Interactive content with embedded examples
- An intelligent chatbot to answer your questions
- Personalized learning paths based on your background
- Support for multiple languages, starting with English and Urdu

Let's begin your journey into Physical AI and humanoid robotics!
